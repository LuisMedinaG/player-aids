Build 1 building
1Choose an empty in a region matching your die. Pay its cost.
2Take any tile from the Building Row and place it on the space. End tile: gain 1 .
3Place your clan marker on the build bonus; gain that influence on the region track. Collect rewards along the path; excess is lost.
4Slide the Building Row and refill from the building board.
Sail 1 ship
1Move 1 ship exactly your die's value downriver. (Count gold spaces only.)
2Collect Visitor rewards (pointed end of tile) from the 4 shore spaces adjacent to where you stop. Empty space = 1 .
3All owners of buildings in those 4 spaces (including you) collect their Owner rewards (flat end of tile). Starting buildings & Imperial Markets grant none.
gained is always in the building's region.
Deliver to 1 customer
1Choose a Customer Card from your hand whose region matches your die. Discard the goods shown on its Order Request.
2Place the card faceup near your clan board. Gain its delivery reward ; ongoing ability activates now.
3Draw 2 Customer Cards, then discard 1 from your hand faceup. Reshuffle discard pile if deck is empty.

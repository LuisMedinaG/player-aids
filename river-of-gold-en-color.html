Setup
1Region Tiles: 1 random scoring tile on each of the 6 Influence Tracks, correct player-count side.
2Mastery Cards: Deal 3 faceup, correct side. Return rest to box.
3Supply: Shuffle customer deck facedown. Pile and trade goods nearby.
4Starting Buildings: Place 3 Imperial Market tiles on the 3 Imperial Market spaces. Place 1 Starting Tile on each shore space marked for the player count.
5Building Row: Place building board near main board. Stack Era 1 + Era 2 tiles facedown (see table). Draw top 4 Era 1 tiles to the building row.

Build 1 Building
1Choose an empty shore space in a region matching your die. Pay its Koku cost. (No ship needed.)
2Take any tile from the Building Row ; place it on the shore space. End tile → gain 1 .
3Place your clan marker on the build bonus; gain that on the region track, collecting rewards you pass. Excess is lost.
4Slide remaining tiles and refill from the building board.

Deliver to 1 Customer
